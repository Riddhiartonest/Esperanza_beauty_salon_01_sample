Taylor Taylor London's award winning Shoreditch hair salon feels as though you have found your very own exclusive members' club.

Resembling the Flat Iron building in New York – and with all the glamour inside, too – our Commercial Street premises is our flagship award-winning unisex hair salon; located in Shoreditch, East London. Just a stone's throw from Spitalfields Market. Inspired by the faded grandeur of 18th century France, Commercial Street's sumptuous Rococo interior is filled with vintage pieces and period finds, to stimulate your senses and make your time at our biggest luxury hair salon in East London a magical experience.

Upon arrival, you will be welcomed by our hairdressing team, who will offer you a cocktail from our authentic studded bar. Then you will be invited to recline upon a vintage chaise longue, where you can soak up the ambience and watch our celebrity hairstylists in action, giving a taste of what's to come with your very own styling to follow: or if you want to explore, our team will guide you through each individually designed room in the salon, so that you will feel as though you have found your very own exclusive members' club.

From the Grand Cutting Room – lit with oversized Murano glass chandeliers to the subterranean golden washrooms, beneath the darkened staircase – we know you will have a visit to Commercial Street that will be truly inspiring for both your hair and your heart.

Colour Director

Even as a child Natalia was inspired by colour and instinctively understood its creative possibilities, it was only natural that her career would lead her to become such a respected colour technician.

Tom Gilling

Tom Gilling

Art Director & Colour Technician

We originally spotted Tom’s talent when we stole him from the North of England where he served a traditional hairdressing apprenticeship from the age of just 14. Working in his spare time while still at school under the mantle of Mark Riley he completed his advanced apprenticeship and rapidly established himself as a senior stylist in record time.

In 2012 Tom won the much coveted title of “ Project X Student of the Year” from the Fellowship for British Hairdressing . Often called the ultimate hairdressing finishing school, Project X aims to select and nurture the very best hairdressing talent in the UK with mentors such as Karine Jackson and hairdressing legend Errol Douglas MBE.

Fast forward to more recent times and Tom has been formally recognized within the hairdressing industry after winning the highly regarded “It List” title of UK Fashionista which is judged by hair icons such as Adam Reed and Luke Hersheson beauty editors from publications such as Vogue and Sunday Times Style.

“Tom was heralded by judges for having an impressive portfolio given his young age, as well as his incredible diversity. In Tom's own words “I adore precision cutting and dressing beautiful hair which makes me a dealer in creative possibility and a trader of joy through great hair .” We couldn’t have said it better ourselves.
