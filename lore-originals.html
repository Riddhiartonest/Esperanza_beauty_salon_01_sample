Løre Originals

The essential product kit bag for your hair

Løre Originals will focus on the integrity of your hair and scalp, with each carefully curated formulation delivering multiple results for effortless styling and long lasting care.

Formulated with our belief in providing a simplified, unisex range of products that provide long lasting integrity and health for every hair type, no matter what your gender. Created by the international team behind Taylor Taylor London our capsule collection harnesses the collective knowledge of acclaimed stylists, clients, British chemists and perfumers and is suitable for all hair types.

Our 6 multi-use formulas are designed to streamline your everyday haircare routine and offer a modern, contemporary range to make “his and hers” products a thing of the past. Deliberately minimal each product will give multiple results depending on how they are used. From our everyday Cleanse + Purify which brilliantly exfoliates, cleanses and nourishes all hair types to our Define + Finish cream which intelligently styles both straight and curly hair, each formula has a unique versatility.
